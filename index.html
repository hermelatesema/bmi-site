Gentle Next Steps

No matter your BMI, small consistent actions make a big difference. Here are a few beginner-friendly ideas.

Start With Movement

Try a short daily walk, light stretching, or a beginner workout video a few times a week. Choose something that feels kind to your body.

Check In With Yourself

Notice your energy, mood, and sleep. Use your BMI as information, not a judgment, and talk to a health professional if you have concerns.

Set Realistic Goals

Focus on simple goals like “move 10 minutes a day” or “drink more water” instead of chasing quick changes.

About This App

Pacific Wellness BMI is a small web app built to help people understand their fitness starting point in a calm and supportive way. It combines a simple BMI calculator with gentle guidance on what to do next.

This tool is educational only and does not replace personalized advice from a doctor or other health professional.
